🕵️ Red Team Simulation

This permission-based simulation is designed to demonstrate ethical hacking and incident response in a controlled environment.

  • Recon & Planning — Define scope, gather intelligence, and simulate threat actor behavior.
  • Initial Access — Use ethical techniques to breach mock perimeter defenses.
  • Persistence & Escalation — Simulate lateral movement and privilege escalation.
  • Exfiltration & Reporting — Document findings, mitigation strategies, and lessons learned.
